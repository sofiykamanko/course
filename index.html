Mechanics

The first section of physics is, obviously, mechanics. We study in depth the movement of bodies in nature and superficially study intermediate topics.

Forces of nature

An extremely important section of physics is the section on the forces of nature. There are many of them: the force of gravity, the force of friction, the force of the normal reaction of the support, the force of elasticity, the force of resistance, the force of Archimedes, the force of pressure, etc. Are you ready to learn a lot of interesting things about each of them?

Electrostatics

How does the light bulb work? How does the current flow? What does the amount of current depend on? You will learn all this while studying the third topic of our course. Many practical experiments related to electrical objects await you.
