February 24 would have been an ordinary day in the life of every Ukrainian, if early in the morning Russian troops had not crossed the borders of our country and struck unexpectedly. For each person, this day was the beginning of a terrible story that is still going on. Every child who faces the realities of war will never forget it and it greatly affects her/his mental health. The founding of our course, unfortunately, is not associated with positive events, but we try to bring at least a little happy emotions into the lives of students along with science.
